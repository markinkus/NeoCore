# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We want NeoCore to be a respectful and technically rigorous community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and specific in feedback.
- Discuss ideas and implementation details, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Repeated hostile behavior after warning.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may edit/remove comments or close contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting
Open a private security-style report via the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
